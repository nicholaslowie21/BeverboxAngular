Beverbox is a beverage subscription box introducing local beverages from different regions around the world every month. With close to a total of 200 countries, travelling the whole world is a costly and time-consuming affair and few will ever have the chance to achieve it. Beverbox closes this gap by delivering the world’s most authentic beverages along with a nicely presented information card right at the customer’s doorstep to experience different culture’s cuisines.
